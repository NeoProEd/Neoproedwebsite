Four professional profiles. One platform.

NeoProEd is built for professionals at different stages — all with a serious intention to grow inside the insurance industry.

💼
Most common
Insurance professionals upskilling
Mid-career professionals in insurance who want to deepen functional expertise, understand the technology landscape, or transition into a leadership track.
Underwriters · Claims managers · Distribution leads · Product managers · Operations heads
🔄
BFSI & cross-industry entrants
Professionals from banking, fintech, healthcare, or technology who see insurance as their next move and want a structured, credible pathway in.
Bank relationship managers · Fintech product managers · Healthcare administrators · Tech sales professionals

Commerce / MBA graduates · Finance graduates · Anyone with 0–3 years experience

Individual agents who want to move from product-selling to consultative, knowledge-led advising — and build a more professional, sustainable practice.
Life agents · General insurance agents · Independent advisors seeking depth
