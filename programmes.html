Common questions

Before you apply

Can I do more than one programme?
Yes — all NeoProEd programmes are designed to be stackable. Completing the Insurtech Professional Program gives you a strong foundation; role-based tracks let you deepen expertise in a specific function. Credits from completed programmes are recognised across the NeoProEd curriculum.
What if I have no prior insurance knowledge?
The flagship Insurtech Professional Program is designed to accommodate participants with no prior insurance background. Module 1 covers Ecosystem & Foundations — giving you everything you need to follow the rest of the programme. A short pre-read document is shared before cohort starts.
Can my employer sponsor my enrolment?
Yes. Many participants are sponsored by their employers. A GST invoice is issued in the company's name. For bulk enrolments (3+ seats), contact us for group pricing. The Organisations page has more detail on how sponsored enrolment works.
Is there an EMI option?
Yes — payment in two instalments is available for the flagship programme. Details are provided at the payment stage after your application is confirmed.
What happens if I miss sessions?
Sessions are recorded and available on the Learnyst platform for 30 days after each session. However, live attendance is strongly encouraged — much of the value comes from cohort discussion. The certificate requires 75% attendance. If you know in advance that you'll miss several sessions, contact us before applying.
Is there a refund policy?
A full refund is available if you withdraw more than 14 days before the cohort start date. Within 14 days of the start date, a 50% refund is offered. No refunds are issued after the programme has started. Full terms are shared at the payment stage.

Ready to join the founding cohort?

Limited seats. High interaction. Direct access to founders. The founding cohort is a rare opportunity — available only once.
