Honest answers

The questions students actually ask us

Will this guarantee me a job?
No — and anyone who promises guaranteed placement in a professional education programme is not being straight with you. What we can promise is structured career support, genuine industry connections, and a credential that gives you a real advantage in interviews. What you do with that advantage is up to you.
I have no insurance background at all. Can I still join?
Absolutely. The Insurtech Professional Program begins with ecosystem foundations — no prior knowledge is assumed. We include a 2-hour Foundation Briefing for students with no insurance exposure, and the programme is designed to take you from zero to genuinely competent in 12 weeks.
I'm still in my final year. Can I join while studying?
Yes. The programme runs in cohort format with live online sessions — designed to fit around an academic schedule. Many participants are final-year students. The residential capstone happens at the end of the programme when scheduling is easier to plan around.
What kinds of companies hire NeoProEd graduates?
Our network spans insurers (life and general), brokers (corporate and retail), insurtechs, GCCs supporting global insurance operations, TPAs, and allied financial services firms. The breadth of insurance as a sector means there are roles across functions — sales, underwriting, claims, operations, technology, analytics, and more.
Is this better than doing a CII or other insurance certification?
Different, not better. CII and similar certifications are exam-based and credential-focused — respected by the industry but text-heavy and not always practically grounded. NeoProEd is practitioner-led and cohort-based, designed to give you functional knowledge and a real network alongside a credential. Many professionals do both — NeoProEd first, then CII to formalise what they know.
