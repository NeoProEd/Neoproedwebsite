How it works

From first conversation to programme delivery

A structured, transparent process — so you know what to expect at every stage.

01
Discovery conversation
We meet your L&D or HR lead to understand your team structure, current capability gaps, and what a successful programme looks like for you
02
Programme design
Our team drafts a bespoke curriculum — role-specific, function-focused, and calibrated to your team's existing knowledge level
03
Faculty matching
We assign faculty with direct experience in the functions being trained — underwriting trainers who have underwritten, claims trainers who have handled claims
04
Delivery & reporting
Programme delivered in-house, cohort, or hybrid format. Attendance, participation, and outcome reporting provided throughout
