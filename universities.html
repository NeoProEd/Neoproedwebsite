Our approach

Every partnership begins with understanding your students, your institution's goals, and what success looks like for both of us.

How a partnership works

From first conversation to student outcomes

A transparent, structured process — with no surprises on either side.

01
Discovery meeting
We meet with your academic coordinator or placement officer to understand your students, your institution's profile, and what a successful programme looks like for you
02
Programme design
We draft a curriculum proposal — session structure, faculty assignment, assessment format, and delivery schedule — aligned to your academic calendar and student level
03
MoU & onboarding
We formalise the partnership with a simple MoU — defining responsibilities, timelines, and outcomes for both parties before any programme begins
04
Delivery & support
Programme delivered as agreed, with NeoProEd managing faculty, content, and student interaction. Attendance and engagement reporting shared throughout
